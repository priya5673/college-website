Message from the Principal

Leadership • Excellence • Vision

Dr. M. Rajamoorthy

M.Sc., M.Phil., B.Ed., Ph.D.

Principal (FAC)

It is my great honour and privilege to address you all as the Principal of this prestigious institution run by the Government of Tamil Nadu.

The Government Arts and Science College, Jayankondam was established in 2020 to provide higher education to first-generation graduates from rural areas.

From 2025, we introduced the Learning from Discussion (LFD) method to promote critical thinking.

We proudly implement the Naan Mudhalvan training programme to equip students with essential skills.

With dedicated faculty and holistic development, our college continues to grow and achieve excellence.
