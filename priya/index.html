Principal

Dr.M.rajamoorthi

Principal, Government Arts and Science College, Jayankondam

Our Principal has been a driving force behind the institution’s growth, fostering academic excellence, discipline, and student-centric learning.

Dr. M. Rajamoorthy, M.Sc., Ph.D., is a distinguished academician and experienced administrator, serving as the Principal (FAC) of Government Arts and Science College, Jayankondam under the Government of Tamil Nadu.

With strong leadership and commitment, he played a key role in establishing the college, expanding undergraduate programmes, and ensuring uninterrupted academic activities, even during the COVID-19 pandemic.

He assumed charge as Principal in May 2025 and continues to guide the institution towards quality education and holistic student development.
